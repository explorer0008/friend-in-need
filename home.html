First Aid Guide
  • CPR (Cardiac Arrest)

    1. Check responsiveness. 2. Call 108. 3. Perform chest compressions (100-120/min). Details

  • Burns

    1. Cool burn with running water (10-15 min). 2. Cover with a clean cloth. 3. Seek medical help. Details

  • Choking

    1. Perform 5 back blows. 2. Perform 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich). 3. Call 108 if unresolved. Details
